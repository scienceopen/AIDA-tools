The optpar format

The optpar format is the format internally used in most functions in the Camera and Starcal toolboxes. It is built as: 

fu fv Alpha Beta Gamma du dv alfa
Where fu is the horizontal focal width, fv the vertical; Alpha, Beta, Gamma are the three rotation angles used by e.g. camera_rot.m; du and dv are the horizontal and vertical shift from the middle of the image of the projection of the optical axis.

The UNITS

Here it is in place to make a short comment on the units. For the rotation angles the parameters are stored in degrees, this is because it is much easier to see whether the results are reasonable limits if given in degrees than in radians.
For the focal widths (fu, fv) and the optical-axis-shifts (du, dv) the best choice appeared to be to use relative units. With this choice it is possible to use the same value for the optical parameters for all re-binning and linear re-sampling of the images - provided that the full image is used.
